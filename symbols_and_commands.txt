Commands:
	D-LS: suppress space for next word
	D*LS: suppress space for last word
	L-R: Lower (decapitalize) last word
	L*R: Unlower (capitalize) last word
	B-FP: backspace
	B*FP: backspace full  considers which has cs considers
	KPA: capitalize next word
	KPA*: capitalize and suppress space for next word
    *URP: uppercase previous word
Symbols:
	Q-K: textual comma
	*R: command comma (all spaces suppessed)
	Q-M: textual question mark
	X-M: textual exclamation mark
